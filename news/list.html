Mailing List

There is an official support mailing list for WML users:

wml-users @ thewml.org

Here you can reach the WML maintainer Denis Barbier and the other people who use WML and which can help you in case of problems. Use this forum to spread your ideas and suggestion, too. Feel free to ask anything WML-related you want. This mailing-list is international and all messages must be posted in English.

Subscription

To subscribe to this Majordomo-controlled mailing list, just send an E-mail to petidomo@thewml.org with only

subscribe wml-users you@domain.dom

in the body (leave the subject empty). After sending this, Majordomo will respond with an authority challenge which has to send back again for security reasons (to avoid that others subscribe you without your ok). Now you are successfully subscribed to this list. To get more details about the functionality of Majordomo, please click here or send us an email.

Please notice that this list allows only subscribers to post because of the existing spamming services. So make sure you're already subscribed before posting and that your E-mail address when posting is the same as used when you subscribed. If it's not possible for you to use exactly this address please mail Ralf S. Engelschall the additional addresses you want to allow posting.
