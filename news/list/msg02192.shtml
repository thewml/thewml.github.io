On Thu, Apr 05, 2001 at 10:49:36AM +0200, Horacio wrote:
> Hi,
> 
> I´m getting a print-ready postscript version of all man pages
> included with wml through
> 
> pod2man --section=n --center="wml manual" foo.pod foo.n
> 
> groff -mandoc -Tps foo.n >foo.ps
> 
> A few other I just get them formatted with a2ps.  But, how can I
> get a (*roff) .ps (or simple .ps) from the mp4h documentation?

The safest solution is to convert .pod documentation to .ps:
under wml_backend/p2_mp4h/doc, run
   make mp4h.pod
and then process this file.
As an exercise, i also wrote a converter to TeXinfo, but never looked
closely how it works. Anyway, you may try
   make mp4h.ps
