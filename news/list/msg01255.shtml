On Thu, Dec 30, 1999, Jan Ulrich Hasecke wrote:

> Since three days I try to connect to www.engellschall.com, but always
> get an errormessage. Is it a proxy-problem, what is very possible or
> is the server down?

The webserver sometimes falls down, but never longer than a few hours,
of course. If you can't connect for more than a day then it's usually
the fault of some routers between you and the webserver. Whether this is
the case you can find out with a "traceroute www.engelschall.com".
