> <PROTECT> does not accept uppercase PASS parameters:

Here's the patch:

*** /usr/local/bin/wml  Mon Jul 16 23:10:50 2001
--- /usr/local/bin/wml~ Thu Jul 12 15:30:47 2001
***************
*** 677,683 ****
          ($prolog, $arg, $body) = ($1, $2, $3);
          #    unquote the attribute
          $arg =~ s|(['"])(.*)\1\s*$|$2|;
!         if ($arg =~ m|pass=([\d,-]*)|i) {
              $p = $1;
              $p =~ s|,||g;
              $p = "1$p" if $p =~ m|^-|;
