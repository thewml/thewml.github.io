> What is MFC?

It's the FreeBSD term from my posting. It stands for "merge from -current" and
means that all changes on the stable branch are bugfixes which are merged from
the trunk to the branch, i.e., all regular commits occur only on the trunk in
CVS and only MFCs are done to enhance/bugfix the stable branch.
