Time to edit your template files (or: how to avoid being smart-tagged)

  • From: Stephan Petersen <nospam@thanx>
  • Date: Wed, 27 Jun 2001 09:42:55 +0200

Hi guys,

Microsoft has meanwhile made the latest release of the Internet
Explorer 6 public beta available for download
(http://www.microsoft.com/windows/ie/preview/default.asp ).

This is the one that supports the infamous "Smart Tags". For those of
you who would like to make sure the only links on your web pages are
the ones *you* put there, you might want to start adding the following
META tag to your wml templates:

<meta name="MSSmartTagsPreventParsing" content="TRUE">

See also 
http://www.microsoft.com/windows/ie/preview/smarttags/default.asp

For an entertaining article on this subject, check out this link:
http://www.zdnet.com/intweek/stories/columns/0,4164,2772297,00.html
It's an excellent read!
