I use something like:

#use wml::bar bar=FooBar

in the including document and then:

$(bar)

in bar.wml will evaluate to FooBar. See
http://www.engelschall.com/sw/wml/docs/backend/wml_p1_ipp.html
under 'Include Variables' for the full syntax.
