On Mon, Jul 17, 2000 at 03:35:09PM +0200, zaucker@ee.ethz.ch wrote:

> Isn't
> 
>       <define-tag endtag=required>
> 
> suppossed to behave the same as
> 
>       <define-container>

Yes.

> The following (stripped down) definition works with <define-container>
> and not with <define-tag>:
> 
> <define-container MAGICK whitespace=delete>
> <perl>
> BEGIN{unshift @INC, "/usr/pack/imagemagick-4.2.9-to/solaris/perl/"};
> use Image::Magick;
> </perl>
> </define-container>

And how did you write it with define-tag?

  <define-tag MAGICK endtag=required whitespace=delete>
  <perl>
  BEGIN{unshift @INC, "/usr/pack/imagemagick-4.2.9-to/solaris/perl/"};
  use Image::Magick;
  </perl>
  </define-tag>

What does not work?
