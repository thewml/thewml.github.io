  • From: nospam@thanx

Hello,

There's a very little 'bug' in wml 2.0.6 wml::std::href,
at the end of define-tag, just before restore, you may find:
--
...</a>
"...
<restore...
--

This adds a space after all links, even with <suck>.

(it looks bad with text links enclosed with brackets)

So, you may remove LF to have:
--
...</a>"...
<restore..
--
