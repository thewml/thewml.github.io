Script to upgrade from WML 1.x to 2.x

  • From: Denis Barbier <nospam@thanx>

Hi,

i eventually wrote a script to help upgrading to WML 2.x.
It is available at
     http://www.engelschall.com/sw/wml/distrib/wml-SNAP/wml_contrib/wml1to2

Its syntax is very simple:
   wml1to2 filename
replaces old constructs by newer ones in `filename'.  See
   perldoc wml1to2
for a detailed list of operations.
