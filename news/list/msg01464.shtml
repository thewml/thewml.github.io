> 2) <sub> is override by built-in tag.
>    How can I write <sub>subscript</sub> ?

In fact, <sub> was defined in mhc but not in mp4h where it is replaced
by <substract>.  But for compatibility reasons, WML defines an alias
sub=substract.  You have 2 solutions:
 a) write <sub*>...</sub*>
or
 b) put <undef sub> at the beginning of your templates.
