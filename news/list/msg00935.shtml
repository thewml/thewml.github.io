On Mon, 6 Sep 1999, Gerfried Fuchs wrote:

> > Take a close look at the line beginning with
> > ** WML:Verbose: splitting to argv
> > If your .wmlrc is read, you should see ``|-I|INCLUDES|'' near the EOL.
> 
>  Let's look at that output:
[...]
> -S /usr/local/lib/wml/include/  -I /INCLUDES  -s 'sys/bootmhc.wml' -s
                                     ^^^^^^^^^
Now, we have to find why INCLUDES has been replaced by /INCLUDES.
Any clue?
Try ``cat -v .wmlrc'' to see whether there are control characters in
your file.

Another hint: when redirecting output, it is a good idea to call wml
with the -q option to suppress printing of '-\|/-\|/-'
