A hint: With the 

./configure --prefix=/usr

or something else like that without the string "wml" in the prefix the 

make test

doesn't complete his run.
If you do this like

./configure --prefix=/some/where/wml
                                 ^^^
                              IMPORTANT

the "make test" runs successfully.
