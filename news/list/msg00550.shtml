       hello.

       well, just a small suggestion: i think <css>..</css> tag
       should be included in the next version of wml, just like
       <javascript>.

       yes, i know that making my own css tag is very easy to do, but
       it should be there by default.

       it should protect the content of <style><!--   --></style> tag
       especially when #id definitions are given, since pass 1 (i
       guess) tries to interpret #myIdDefinition and strips hash
       marks '#' from the beginning. (must use <protect> to avoid
       that)

       <css> -> <style type="text/css"><!-- ....  --></style>
