[ANNOUNCE] WML 1.99.8

  • From: Denis Barbier <nospam@thanx>
  • Date: Tue, 18 Apr 2000 01:05:18 +0200
Hi folks,

another beta-release is out and its name is WML-1.99.8

You may ask why it is not called WML 2.0 yet, and the answer is that
this version introduces many changes in compilation mechanism to fix
long-awaited compilation problems on FreeBSD.  This version has been
tested on Linux boxes (with perl 5.005 and 5.6.0), FreeBSD 3.1 and
SunOS 5.6.
I am very interested if this version fails compiling on other platforms.
