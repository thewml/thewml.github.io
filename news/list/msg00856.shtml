Minor fix for command line options

  • From: Simon Burr <nospam@thanx>
  • Date: Fri, 16 Jul 1999 15:59:13 +0100 (BST)
Whilst playing with wml today I came across the following - whilst the
wml usage info says that the safe mode can be used by giving -s or --safe,
the getopts function is told that the options are -s or -speedup. The following
patch, against the current CVS tree, fixes this,

*** wml.src-ORIG        Fri Jul 16 15:52:16 1999
--- wml.src     Fri Jul 16 15:52:34 1999
***************
*** 129,135 ****
          "p|pass=s@",
          "W|passoption=s@",
          "M|depend:s" ,
!         "s|speedup",
          "v|verbose:i",
          "q|quiet",
          "V|version:i",
--- 129,135 ----
          "p|pass=s@",
          "W|passoption=s@",
          "M|depend:s" ,
!         "s|safe",
          "v|verbose:i",
          "q|quiet",
          "V|version:i",
