> and tried to #use wml::std::lang (also wml::all, but didn't work), the
> languages are also defined in the layout file, but it doesn't work.

> The error msg:
> ** Slice:Error: no existing slice matches `LANG_*'
> ** WML:Break: Error in Pass 9 (rc=1).

Sure, try with -v# where # is the verbosity level (between 1 and 9)

Your error message means that you did not define any language with
<lang:new>
