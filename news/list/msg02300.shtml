BUG: <PROTECT> does not accept uppercase PASS parameters:

  • From: Axel Beckert <nospam@thanx>
  • Date: Mon, 16 Jul 2001 23:04:53 +0200
Hi!

There is a little bug in WML: <PROTECT> does not accept uppercase PASS
parameters:

 1| <protect pass=2>
 2| <define-tag foo>
 3| <: print "foo\n"; :>
 4| </define-tag>
 5| </protect>
 6|
 7| <protect PASS=2>
 8| <define-tag bar>
 9| <: print "bar\n"; :>
10| </define-tag>
11| </protect>

results in

1| <define-tag foo>
2| foo
3| </define-tag>
4|
5| <define-tag bar>
6| <: print "bar\n"; :>
7| </define-tag>      

Tested with WML 2.0.7 on Solaris 2.7, PERL 5.6.1:
