Please do not change this particular slice.src, it was generated out of the
original sources. I've placed the "assembled" slice program in the WML source
tree instead of the full sources, because the original intention was that
Slice is always maintained externally as a stand-alone package. So when you
want to maintain a patched version of Slice inside the WML source tree, please
replace wml_backend/p9_slice/* with the full-size Slice distribution files.
Then you can patch the slice_xxx.pl sources in a cleaner way. Thanks.
