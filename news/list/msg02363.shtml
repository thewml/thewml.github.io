On 15-Aug-01 Stephan Petersen wrote:
> What I want to do is to create both a frames and a non-frames version

Use $man wml_tutorial to get used to output-slices and divertion.
That's what you would need to get to a solution.

With the slices you generate the two different html-files you
need. The divertion-mechanism will fill in your date into
the slices.
