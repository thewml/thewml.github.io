On Thu, 18 Nov 1999 MULLERY@uk.ibm.com wrote:

> Hi,
> 
> I want to include several META tags in the HEAD section of my documents. How can
> I do that? The PAGE tag doesn't include support for that (INFO is not what I'm
> looking for).

Below is an idiot example

   #use wml::std::page

   <page>
   <head>
   <meta name="Author" content="me">
   <meta name="Copyright" content="me too">
   </head>
   <title>My home page</title>

   Hello
