Hi,
I beleive wml::fmt::verbatim should also escape the backslash, shouldn't
it? In the following example the newline between test2 and test3 ist
removed:


#use wml::std::page
#use wml::fmt::verbatim
<page>
<verbatim>
test
test2 \
test3
</verbatim>


I tested wml-2.0.2, wml-1.7.4 and wml-2.0.3200009161200.
