On Wed, 10 Mar 1999, Tobias Oetiker wrote:

> apropos features, I was wondering why there was no
> 
> #define something=blabla
> 
> in the ipp pass .... analog to -Dsomething=blabla

Tobias, maybe it's because ipp means ``Include Pre-Processor''?

I think Ralf added the -D option when he used ipp in conjonction with
wml, but he never implemented #define.

The -D option is more general, because variables are defined for all
passes. I am not sure it will be clear to the end-user that the variable
defined with #define becomes meaningless after the first pass, so it is
not a good idea (IMHO, of course :o).
